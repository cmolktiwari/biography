Biography

Childhood and Early Adulthood.

Born in September 1952 in the remote village of Narani, District Fatehpur, I embarked on a challenging yet remarkable journey. My early education took place in the humble village of Basfara, where I walked barefoot and sat on the floor in a primary school. Despite the odds, I secured admission to a secondary school in Kapil, two miles away from my village, continuing my barefoot journey. In 1967, I excelled in high school at BNSD College Kanpur, receiving a scholarship for my outstanding performance. However, a stumble in 1968 due to class bunking and cinema visits led to academic failure, but I quickly rectified my behavior and focused on my dream of becoming a medical professional. My hard work paid off as I secured the 12th position in the province for medical studies. Graduating as a medical doctor in 1976, I further specialized in orthopedic surgery and began teaching tuitions to make ends meet. Joining UPPMS as a gazetted officer in 1979, I faced numerous challenges in rural postings with limited amenities. Yet, in 1983, my life took a turn for the better when I got posted in Kanpur, marking the start of brighter days ahead.

Highlights of my career in medicine.

In 1981, I began my journey in Kanpur and gradually climbed the ranks until I achieved the position of health officer in NOIDA Authority by 1996. Tragically, in 1997, I experienced the heartbreaking loss of my newborn son, leading to a severe mental breakdown. Overwhelmed by grief, I made the regrettable decision to leave my job, which I later realized was a significant mistake. Unfortunately, during this challenging time, I found little support and encountered jealousy-driven misinformation from others. However, in 1998, I came to understand my misstep and resolved to reclaim my lost mental well-being. With determination and hard work, I successfully regained my position as a respected officer. I reached the pinnacle of my career when I became CMO in 2007, and from that point on, I never looked back. In 2011, an unfounded and fabricated allegation surfaced against me, catching me off guard while I was away. But my innocence and unwavering confidence allowed me to overcome this obstacle, with the help of divine intervention. Throughout my life, numerous miracles have occurred, and I attribute these blessings to the unwavering support and assistance I've received from God. Today, as I reflect on my life, I am grateful for the good name and fame that accompanied my dedicated service. I bow before God with deep gratitude for all the times He has helped and continues to guide me.
